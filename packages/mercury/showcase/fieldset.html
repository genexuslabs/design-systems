fieldset

A primary HTML fieldset is a fundamental form element that groups related fields together, enhancing the organization and readability of a form. It typically includes a legend to provide a clear label for the grouped elements, making the form more accessible and user-friendly.

Using a fieldset with a legend element improves form accessibility and organization by grouping related elements with a descriptive title, enhancing both usability and visual clarity.
