buttons group

A button group is a collection of buttons grouped together, usually for related actions. It helps organize multiple actions in a cohesive way, providing users with clear options. Button groups can be used for navigation, formatting, or other sets of actions, enhancing the user interface by making interactions intuitive and accessible.

Displays a group of filled buttons on a flat surface. These buttons have solid background colors, making them visually prominent and ideal for primary actions.

Displays a group of outlined buttons on a flat surface. These buttons have borders without a solid background, making them less visually intrusive and suitable for secondary actions.
